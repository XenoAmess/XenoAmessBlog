#### 3.1 对于不能过的版本16上experimental: true，使其不停止其余流水

```yaml
    strategy:
      matrix:
        java: [ 8, 11 ]
        experimental: [false]
        include:
          - java: 16
            experimental: true
          - java: 17-ea
            experimental: true
```
